About me

I am Amber Snowden, a sophomore communication arts student at Cab Calloway. I am a Graphic Designer and Coder, as well as a Musician and Singer. On this website is a selection of a few of my complete projects.

Portfolio

Grateful Mashup

This hand-drawn letterform is a mix of the typefaces Bodoni Poster Compressed and Interstate Bold Compressed.

A tiny home

This home inside a mailbox was a surrealist project I made for my Foundations of Digital Design Class in 2023, with the goal of showing contrasting forces or Composition in a Dreamlike reality. It's an affordable starter home for the current housing market!

Cat serenading aggressive birds

This image was spawned from the verbiage project of my Foundations class, combining random words to make up wacky situations. All images were sourced from 25+ year old National Geographic magazines, with the exception of the digitally created acoustic guitar.

Grateful Dead Poster

A digitally created poster for a Grateful Dead show that happened before I was born. The show is featured in their live album, Dick's Pick's Volume 7. One of my personal favorite projects, the purple dreamcatcher represents the pristine yet real sound of the record.
